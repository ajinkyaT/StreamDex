# Streamdex Final Submission Write-Up

Streamdex is a personal broadcast layer for the MCP ecosystem. It turns trusted inputs like broker data, news, docs, repos, calendars, and internal tools into short, polished video briefings that are faster to consume than reading across tabs and dashboards.

The product is built around a simple idea: users should own the sources, own the ranking, and own the format. Streamdex gathers context from the tools they already trust, selects the most relevant signal, and transforms it into a concise video update that can be watched in under a minute.

What Streamdex demonstrates:

- Source-connected briefing generation from user-owned tools and data.
- Personalized summarization and ranking instead of generic AI output.
- Video-first delivery for quick daily consumption.
- Recurring automations for morning, evening, or on-demand updates.
- A portable workflow that can live inside Codex and other Streamdex-compatible surfaces.

Why it matters:

- It reduces information overload without asking users to give up control of their sources.
- It makes important context easier to watch, share, and act on.
- It reframes AI video as a utility layer for real-world workflows, not just a creative demo.

In the final demo, the sample videos highlight how Streamdex can adapt to different kinds of briefings while keeping the experience short, focused, and practical.
