# Streamdex Final Demo Video Reference

This folder is a compact creative handoff for the final hackathon demo.

Contents:
- `voiceover-script.md` - final spoken script in line-break form
- `brand-direction.md` - theme, color system, typography, and visual guidance
- `streamdex-logo-hero.svg` - presentation-ready logo on dark background
- `streamdex-logo-mark.svg` - transparent logo mark for overlays and compositing

Core idea:
- Streamdex helps people take back control of their attention.
- It turns trusted connected tools into personalized video briefings.
- The visual theme is `signal over noise`.

Current demo assets:
- Primary sample briefing: a news-style explainer about the npm supply chain attack
- Meme beat: `TL;DR` meme page stored in `assets/`

Creative north star:
- Start in chaos.
- Transition into clarity.
- End with ownership: `Your sources. Your ranking. Your format. Your stream.`
